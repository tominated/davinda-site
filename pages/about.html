About Davinda Innovations

Davinda Innovations is a new Australian company established 4th November 2011. The company directors are David and Belinda Brunoli (hence the company name, DAVid belINDA).

Belinda is a multi-skilled and business savvy operator with many years experience in general business and finance administration. In more recent times and whilst successfully managing a family of three children, Belinda operates a small part time drink and snack vending machine business.

After graduating as an Electrical Engineer, David kicked off a successful career in Sales and Management in the controls and automation space for almost 14 years. This was followed by approximately 2 years in the records and document management sector. A further 10 years has been involved with Sales and management in the outsource customer communications environment. David's other formal qualifications include a Graduate Diploma in Business Administration and a Certificate 4 in Building.

Davinda Innovations represents the next chapter and an opportunity to fulfil a long term desire to take a number of ideas, which in some cases have been on the drawing board for many years, and turn them into saleable and useful items. There are several areas where product design and construction will be directed. In broad terms these will be product development to improve a family's way of life.
